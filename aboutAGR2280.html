About AGR2280

The Return of AG

Anti-Gravity racing has just been brought back after a rather abrupt ending in 2175 due to lack of safety to pilots. This time around ships have to be checked thoroughly to ensure pilots can survive everything from the extreme shifts in G-force to having their ship blow up. Before the offical tracks open again, test events are going to be held from 2279 - 2280 on the streets of New York to ensure that the sport is safer then ever!
